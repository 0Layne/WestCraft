Our Custom Home Building Process

At WestCraft, we prioritize the understanding of the process & the involvement of your project

  • Vision
  • Floor Planning
  • Consultation
  • Final Activity

Vision

We begin by understanding your vision, lifestyle, location, and desired investment for your custom home.

  • ✔ In-Depth Consultations: We learn what inspires you—architecture, art, travel, or legacy. These conversations become the foundation of your home's concept.
  • ✔ Site Analysis: Evaluating terrain, light, and environment to optimize placement and presence. We study the land as carefully as the layout.
  • ✔ Lifestyle-Centric Planning: Your routines, guests, and ambitions shape the framework. Every space is crafted with intention and relevance.

Floor Planning

If need be, we can help resource designers and architects to develop a custom floor plan tailored to your needs, space, and aesthetic.

  • ✔ Builder Input Early On: We collaborate with your architect or designer from the beginning—ensuring ideas translate smoothly into structure and execution.
  • ✔ Constructability & Feasibility: We review plans with a builder’s lens—catching costly oversights early and providing realistic input on timelines, materials, and layout.
  • ✔ Unified Vision: Our role is to build in sync with the design intent—maintaining continuity between your architect’s plans and what’s delivered on site.

Construction

This is where the vision takes shape. We break ground and manage every phase of the build—coordinating trades, scheduling inspections, and ensuring everything is completed to code and craftsmanship standards.

  • ✔ Hands-on Supervision: We’re on site throughout the project, making sure each stage is done right and meets code.
  • ✔ Seamless Workflow: We coordinate the work between crews, suppliers, and inspectors to keep everything on schedule.
  • ✔ Built to Last: From framing to finishes, we hold every detail to a high standard of quality and craftsmanship.

Final Activity

Completion isn’t our finish line—it’s where our long-term commitment begins. We’re here beyond move-in to support your home, your satisfaction, and the legacy of your investment.

  • ✔ Detailed Final Walkthroughs: We review every detail with you, making sure craftsmanship meets expectations and that your home functions as intended.
  • ✔ Continued Availability: Whether it’s seasonal maintenance advice or minor adjustments, we stay connected and ready to help.
  • ✔ Legacy-Driven Craftsmanship: Built to endure—our projects reflect timeless construction methods designed to last for decades to come.
